void Braincase.GanttChart.IProjectManager< T, R >.Join ( T  part1,
T  part2 
)

Join part1 and part2 in a split task into a single part represented by part1, and part2 will be deleted from the ProjectManager. The resulting part will have a duration total of the two parts. Schedule of other parts will be packed according to direction of join. If the join will result in only one part remaining, the split task will merge instead.

Parameters
part1The part to keep in the ProjectManager after the join completes successfully.
part2The part to join into part1 and be deleted afterwards from the ProjectManager.

Split the specified task into consecutive parts part1 and part2.

Parameters

part1New Task part (1) of the split task, with the start time of the original task and the specified duration value.
